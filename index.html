About our Team

  
We are a team of 8th, 9th and 10 graders who are fun loving, passionate and inspired to compete in robotics tournament. We participate in competitions by building a robot as a team of software and Hardware interests. Our team is believes in giving back to the community with various outreach programs by teaching elementary aged students about building and coding. In a nutshell, our goal is to build a robot not only to win competitions but also spark ideas for others about technological path.
